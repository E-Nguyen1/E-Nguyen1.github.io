WIZARD WORLD

QUIZZLES ABOUT WIZARD

Hello! Welcome to wizard world! Wizard World is a story trivia game featuring Harry Potter and Philosopher's Stone. There are 3 phases that you have to overcome: The Invitation Letter, Christmas Miracles, and The Philosopher Stone. By the end of the adventure, if you have conquered all of the challenges by answering all the questions, you will be accepted by Hogwarts to graduated the first year in Hogwarts to become a true wizard. If you did not get it done perfectly, it is alright, you will be giving multiple chances to retake the questions until you are confident to move on to second year at Hogwarts. Okay, LET'S GO!
